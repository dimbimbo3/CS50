eCommerce Storefront

| Languages/Frameworks: HTML/CSS, SQL, PHP, Bootstrap |
| Project Type: Solo, Class: Database Systems |
| FALL 2021 |

  • Created an ecommerce site for making purchases and managing employees, orders, products and stock.
  • Implemented an account-based system (using hashed passwords) for customer and employee accounts, as well as one for the owner.
  • Customer accounts can search for items, add them to cart, change their quantity, place orders, view their account's previous order history, and alter any of their account info.
  • Employee accounts can view basic information pertaining to employees, a list of the store's existing products, and all placed orders. In addition to these, the owner's account can view detailed employee info, hire new employees and fire current ones, change the manager of a department, add products to the store's inventory, change the current stock of an item, and cancel any previously placed orders.
