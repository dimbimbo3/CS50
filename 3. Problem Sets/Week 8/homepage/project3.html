GUI Game (Connect 4)

| Languages/Frameworks: Java, Swing, Abstract Window Toolkit |
| Project Type: Solo, Class: Intro. to Computer Science II (Java II) |
| FALL 2018 |

  • Turned the Connect Four board game into a GUI-based experience for two players.
  • At the beginning of the game, the players are prompted to decide whether Player 1 or 2 is red or black.
  • The players are then presented with an empty 6x7 board with buttons above each column, allowing them to take turns dropping their pieces in.
  • The winner of the game is ultimately decided when four pieces of the same color are connected horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. (If all of the spaces become filled without a winner then it is a draw.)
